**Always use "duration" terminology:**
- ‚úÖ Duration start
- ‚úÖ Duration end (NOT "expiration date")

### Name & Meaning
- **LiberoVino** = "Liberate Wine"

### Core Messaging

**Revolutionary Concept:**

- They have **freedom** to purchase when ready (within limits)
- They control their **own schedule and quantity**
- No forced shipments or predetermined schedules

**Use "liberate/liberation" everywhere appropriate:**
- "Liberate your wine buying"
- "Liberation from scheduled shipments"  
- "Free yourself to buy on your terms"
- "Liberated wine club experience"

2. **Focus on Benefits, Not Restrictions**

   - ‚úÖ "You have N months to enjoy your tier benefits"

## üéÅ Incentive Messaging

### Primary Motivators

All communication should center around:

1. **Tier Advancement**
   - "Move up to unlock better discounts"
   - "Higher tiers = more savings"
   - "Advance your tier to increase benefits"

2. **Loyalty Points** (after 1 year)
   - Points initiation at 1-year membership
   - Accumulation rewards long-term loyalty
   - "Your loyalty pays off - literally"

3. **Duration Extension**
   - "Extend your duration with each purchase"
   - "The more you buy, the longer your benefits last"
   - "Push your duration end date further out"

### The Core Difference

**Traditional Clubs:**
- Members buy **minimum required** only
- Everything predetermined by winery
- Scheduled shipments create wine overload
- #1 reason for opt-out: "Too much wine"

**LiberoVino:**
- Members buy **when they're ready**
- No attention needed on purchase tracking
- No forced schedules
- Extended duration = less worry about "when to buy next"
- **Freedom eliminates the #1 opt-out reason**

### Messaging Examples

‚úÖ **Good:**
- "Buy wine when your cellar needs it, not when we schedule it"
- "Your next purchase extends your membership - stay liberated longer"
- "No surprise shipments, ever"
- "The more you buy, the higher your tier and the more you save"

‚ùå **Avoid:**
- "Your membership expires on..."
- "You're required to purchase..."
- "Club shipment scheduled for..."
