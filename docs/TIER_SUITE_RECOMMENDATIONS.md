## Tier Suite Catalog

### Suite A: Traditional Replacement

**Best For:**
- Wineries converting from traditional clubs
- Familiar structure for existing members
- Gradual transition to flexibility

**Winery Profile:**
- Price Range: $20-60/bottle
- Current: Traditional club (3/6/12 bottle tiers)
- Goal: Easy transition, maintain familiarity
- Audience: Mass market to mid-premium

**Tier Configuration:**

#### Bronze (Entry Tier)
```yaml

Min Purchase: 3 bottles ($60 minimum)
Discount: 15%
Loyalty: None
Duration: 3 months
Promotions:
  - 15% off all wines (auto-apply)

Customer Experience:
  - Buy 3+ bottles â†’ Save 15%
  - Renew every 3 months with another purchase

```

```yaml

## Suite Selection Guide

### Decision Matrix

| If Winery Has... | Recommend Suite | Why |
|-----------------|----------------|-----|
| Traditional 3/6/12 bottle club | **Suite A** | Easy migration, familiar structure |
| Active tasting room, casual customers | **Suite B** | Loyalty-first, repeat visits |
| High-end wines ($60+/bottle) | **Suite C** | Premium tiers, VIP experiences |
| Wide customer base, diverse prices | **Suite D** | Flexibility for all customer types |
| No current club | **Suite B or D** | Start fresh with modern approach |

### Quick Questionnaire

```
1. What's your average bottle price?
   a) Under $25 â†’ Suite B or D
   b) $25-60 â†’ Suite A or D
   c) Over $60 â†’ Suite C

2. What's your primary sales channel?
   a) Tasting room â†’ Suite B
   b) Direct shipping â†’ Suite A or C
   c) Both equally â†’ Suite D

3. What's your business goal?
   a) Acquire new customers â†’ Suite B (low entry)
   b) Increase frequency â†’ Suite B (loyalty focus)
   c) Reward top spenders â†’ Suite C (premium tiers)
   d) All of the above â†’ Suite D (comprehensive)

4. Current club structure?
   a) Traditional club â†’ Suite A (transition)
   b) No club â†’ Suite B or D (modern start)
   c) Allocation/mailing list â†’ Suite C (premium)

5. Customer base size?
   a) Small (<1,000) â†’ Suite C (fewer, higher value)
   b) Medium (1,000-5,000) â†’ Suite A or D
   c) Large (5,000+) â†’ Suite B or D (volume focus)
